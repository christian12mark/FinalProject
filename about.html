Mission

We commit ourselves to follow Jesus Christ and the Gospel values, with the Carmelian- spirit of Prayer, Compassion and Prophetic Action as we continually strive for quality education in the service of the church and society.

Vission

We, the catholic school of the Prelature of Infanta,inspired by the Blessed Trinity, strive towards the holisic formation of the person and the society in communion with the church, the community and the family under the maternal care of Our Lady of Mount Carmel.

History

The Mount Carmel College of Baler (Kolehiyong Monte Carmelo ng Baler in Filipino), commonly referred to as MCC Baler or MCCB, is the oldest Catholic mission school in the Philippine province of Aurora.
